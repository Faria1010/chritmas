about us

The concept of the show came to me in the 1980's while listening to other shows that were the same year after year. In fact one year I opened up the show, and the CD's had coffee stains on them! I decided then that an annually updated Christmas show would one day be my goal.That was 18 years ago! The show is written, hosted, and produced by myself. Every year tho I lean on a few others for some much appreciated assistance. People like Joel Denver of All Access play a valueable part in the shows success. As well as Jim Sharp/Pro Audio Productions for his "Mastering" of the show. 4 years ago we welcomed back Robin Marshall as the "First Lady" of Christmas Across the Lands.
